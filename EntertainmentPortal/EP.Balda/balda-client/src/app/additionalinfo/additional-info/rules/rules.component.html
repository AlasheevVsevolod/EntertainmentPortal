Game rules

  • Each turn you can add only one letter on the field.
  • The letter should form a new word.
  • Syllables can be only vertical or horisontal diagonal words are prohibited so the new letter can form a syllable with the letter above, below or on the sides of it.
  • The goal is to fill the field with words.
  • You can't repeat the words, if someone wrote room, you can't write room as well even if it will be the word in the another part of the field.
  • The winner the person who will have more total points by the end of the game.
  • One point is given for one letter of words you add.
  1. Shows whose move it is now.
  2. There you can choose a letter to add to the playing field.
  3. Press this button after you choose letters of word correctly.
  4. Press this button if you choose some letters of word wrong.
  5. Press this button if you want to end this game.
